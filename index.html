About Me

I am a strong AI researcher and EAI enthusiast.

In my undergraduate studies, I am pursuing mathematical computer science and neuroscience. I have loved mathematics since elementary school, and I believe we can improve society by democratizing artificial intelligence (AI). Through neuroscience, I hope to bridge the gap between human and artificial intelligence.

My other interests include affective computing, social robotics, computational biology, and extemporaneous speaking. I advocate for domestic violence awareness.
